Guide for Beat Saber downgrading
A guide for downgrading Beat Saber and settings up BMBF
Prequesites
1. You got Beat Saber bought on your Quest
2. You got a PC
First downgrade your game
Head to the downgrading site and download any version you want.
If you get page not found then log in on oculus.com with your account which owns Beat Saber
Then install the apk you downloaded via Sidequest.
Now pick the version specific guide
Downgraded to 1.16.4 and up?
You are good to go. You no longer need to follow this guide.

Downgraded to 1.12.1 - 1.13.2?
head to this section and follow it

on any other version?
Follow the steps below
everything other version
Important info
You won't be able to load songs via BMBF as neither does BMBF support song loading nor do any mods exists to load them at the time of writing.
You probably won't be able to find mods for the version you have as well because the mods will need to be in the qmod format.
If you do not want to mod simply stop following the guide gere Download and install the latest BMBF version from bmbf.dev/stable
You can then follow the BMBF setup. For that simply open BMBF and follow all the steps.
You are now ready to play
Backing up your old BMBF installation. Skip if you didn't mod before
Open Sidequest on your PC and click the folder icon at the top right.
Click the save icon next to BMBFData and save it to some safe location.
Afterwards press the X next to BMBFData to delete it
Installing BMBF
Install the BMBF version for your game version from bmbf.dev/stable.
e. g. the release with Update to Beat Saber v1.13.2 as title
Open BMBF in your Quest and follow the modding process
Restoring songs (WIP)
In Sidequest click the folder icon and go to ModData/Mods/SongLoader and save Customlevels
Download This program, unzip the zip and start the executable.
Drag and drop the customlevels folder you saved into the program and press enter.
After it's done it'll open a folder in explorer. Open BMBFData/CustomSongs in Sidequest and drag and drop all folders in the folder it opened into Sidequest. Finally hit reload songs folder in the tools tab of BMBF to load all songs and sync your game.
