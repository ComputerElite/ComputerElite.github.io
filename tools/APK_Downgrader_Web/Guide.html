Guide for APK Downgrader - Web
Based on Hack64's Web Patcher
Step 1: Backup your Beat Saber APK
Open Sidequest and conenct your Quest. Make sure there's a green dot at the top left. Make sure you got a fresh copy of your game from the oculus store
Then press the 9 dots at the top right (1), click the cog next to Beat Saber (2) and hit Backup APK (3)
Step 2: Download the patch
Open the APK Downgrader - Web. You'll see a dropdown with available versions (1). Select the version you want to downgrade to and hit Find (2). Then download the patch which is showing your Source version (3). Note: Multiple downgrades may be shown for some versions. This means you have to downgrade the downgraded apk with the next downgrade and so on until you get to your desired version.
Simply click download once mega opens (4)
Step 3: Select your Beat Saber APK
On the APK Downgrader click choose file (or something similar) next to APK (1) and then paste (if you are on windows) %appdata%/SideQuest/backups/com.beatgames.beatsaber/apks into the address bar (2)
Select the latest APK (which is usually at the top or you just check the date modified; 3) and hit open (4)
Step 4: Select the patch
Click choose file (or something similar) next to Patch (1).
Find the file you downloaded in step 2 which should be in your download folder (2) and hit open (3)
Step 5: Downgrade the APK
Now simply press Downgrade (1) and a downgraded APK should download after a few second to half a minute.
Step 6: Uninstall Beat Saber on your Quest
Open Sidequest and conenct your Quest. Make sure there's a green dot at the top left.
Then press the 9 dots at the top right (1), click the cog next to Beat Saber (2) and Uninstall App (3)
Step 7: Install downgraded Beat Saber
Now finally press Install APK from folder on computer in SideQuest (1), find your downgraded apk (2), hit open (3) and your downgraded APK should then install to your Quest.
Hooray you downgraded your game. If you have any further questions as on The Beat Saber Legacy Group Discord server
